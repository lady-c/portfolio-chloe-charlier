A story of Power

A Story of Power is the story of Z6-PO, a robot created to help humans getting their power back! He will fight, learn, and grow… Some say that an unlimited power source exists somewhere in these lands… Could he picture everything he could do with that? Not for now. But remember… He’ll learn.

Game developed for the Hackthemidlands 5.0 Gamejam, hosted from the 22nd to the 25th of October 2020.
The theme was Unlimited Power.

I was narrative designer on the game and wrote the story and dialogues. Our custom dialogue system allows us to divide the game in four steps, with dialogues changing at each one of them, for all the NPCs.

The game is a 3D Adventure.

Programmer and artist: Christina Charlier

3D artist: Kieran Wilson



(game only playable on the browser, using a mouse & keyboard)
