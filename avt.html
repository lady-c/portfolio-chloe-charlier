Audi Visual Training

AVT (Audi Visual Training) was a very interesting challenge because it isn't really a game. It can be defined as a "gamified training program" aimed at an audience that likely doesn't consist of gamers.

I worked on this project as a UI Designer and UX consultant. My role involved simplifying some UIs and improving their visibility and clarity.

An additional difficulty was that the entire documentation was in German, as the app is only available in German.
