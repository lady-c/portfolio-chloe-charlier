Only Human

Only Human is the story of a 10 y/o being the only human in a robot magic school — yep, you read that right! Will you manage to decipher the ancient texts and perform better than your robot classmates?

The game was developed for the InkJam2021.
The jam was hosted from the 27th to the 30th of August 2021, on itch.io.

I was the Writer and Puzzle Designer. I wrote everything in Ink, and then the story was implemented into Godot by Christina, the team's programmer.

The game is a Narrative Game with some puzzles. The choices are important and will give you a different narrative experience. The resolution of one puzzle in particular will give you a secret to unlock!

Developer and artist: Christina Piberger.



(game only playable on the browser, using a mouse)
