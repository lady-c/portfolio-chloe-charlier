Game Dev Tycoon

Design blockbuster games, build your studio from the ground up and become part of retro video game history in this super-satisfying business manager simulation. New features exclusive to this Netflix edition let you develop games based on movies and leverage livestreaming to grow your fanbase.

To succeed as the founder of a game development empire in this creative sim, you'll need to experiment, make smart decisions and use your time wisely. Upgrade your skills as a developer with each release and win fans around the world to progress toward the dream of becoming a true tycoon.

Developed by Greenheart Games (original PC version), in partnership with Rarebyte (Mobile and Switch port). Published by Netflix.

I worked on the Netflix version, which is the version of the game only available on Netflix ( mobile devices).

My main role on the project was Narrative Designer/Game Writer, but I am also responsible for part of the new UIs.

Bullet point list of my work:
• Narrative: Establishing the list of licences to use and writing the short funny descriptions for most of them.
• Narrative: Helped in the design of several of the licenced-games related quests and writing the texts and reviews.
• Narrative: Establishing the list of Streamers/Youtubeurs to include, changing their name to a parody version and establishing their prefered (in-game) game type and audience.
• UI: Designed the catalogue page for licences.
• UI: Partly designed the "Chose a streamer" marketing page.
• UI: Designed the "Watch stream" screen.
• Bug Fixing: Helped with bug fixing in the UI (original and new content).
