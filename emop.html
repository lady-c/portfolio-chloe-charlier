Emergency Operator

Welcome to EMERGENCY Operator, the ultimate 911 location-based game that turns your hometown into a thrilling playground of rescue missions! If you've ever wanted to step into the shoes of a firefighter, police officer, or any other first responder, this casual yet engaging game is for you. Jump into the action! Answer emergency calls in real time!

Developed by Promotion Software, in partnership with Rarebyte. Published by Promotion Software.

This project was the first time I worked directly with our client/partner without involving someone more senior from my company. It was definitely a very formative experience, and I greatly enjoyed collaborating with the other UI Designer and Game Designers from Promotion Software.

Most of the time, to accomplish my tasks, I received very rough concepts from the UI Designer at Promotion Software. From there, I would create clean and precise mockups to pass on to our programmer for implementation. Occasionally, I was provided with only some ideas and directions and had to create concepts from scratch. After a few months on the project, I had a comprehensive understanding of it and its design, and I became the primary person our programmers consulted for assistance.

Bullet points list of UIs I have worked on:
• Design of building/purchase menus
• Design of in-game monetization shop elements
• Main quests pop-ups
• In-game special events pop-ups
• Player temporary and permanent ban pop-up
• Player profile
• Quests progression screens
• Entire design of the graphics (icons) and UIs for the weather system (late game update using the real-life weather of the player's location)
