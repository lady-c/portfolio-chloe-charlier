The Highway Story

It's a hot Sunday morning, the sun has risen just about an hour ago. Your mom woke you up early because you have to attend a family lunch at your uncle's. You don't really know much about him, and you don't care much for him either. But he's family and your mom insisted for you to come with her. You know it's going to be a long drive and you're tired from last night's gaming session. It was Just... one... more... turn... all over again. But you needed that to take your mind off of your issues. unfortunately, you're failing your math class and you will have to attend summer classes to be able to get into the next year, and you didn't build up the courage to tell your mom yet. At the same time, your mom has been distant and moody lately. You don't really know what's up with her, but she's been snapping at you a lot. Which is why you had a terrible argument this morning. You're both in a terrible mood. Sitting at the back of the car, you've been on the road for a few minutes only but you can already feel the tension.

The Highway Story is a chill short feel-good story. This is my first solo Ink project, and the story is quite simple.
My main objectif was to toy with the variables and the CSS.
Depending on your choices, it can be quite short.
There is 11 endings.



Game playable in browser and on mobile.
